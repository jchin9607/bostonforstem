Boston For Stem

A student-led organization committed to providing underprivileged communities in Boston with access to STEM education and resources.

Get to know us

Boston for Stem is an organization led by high school students. In the coming future, we will offer free STEM classes, hackathons, competitions, donations and much more.

Our Mission

In low-income and poor areas, children are most likely to have fewer resources for STEM. At this organization, we strive to change that.
