Boston For Stem

A student-led organization committed to helping struggling communities in Boston with access to STEM education and resources.

  • Learn more

Get to know us

Boston for Stem is an organization led by highschool students. In the coming future, we will offer free STEM classes, hackathons, competitions, donations and much more.

Our Mission

In low-income and poor areas, children are most likely to have fewer resources for STEM. At this organization, we strive to change that.

What we do

Here are all events we plan to do.

Fundraisers

Fundraisers are events that help raise money for this organization to give back.

Competitions

Competitions are events where many people compete to win something. We plan to host hackathons and much more.

Classes

We plan to host STEM classes to teach children how to code, program robots, create websites, and much more.

Donating

We plan to donate to other organizations and charities that have the same cause and donate phones and computers to give back centers
